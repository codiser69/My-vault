# Mahajanpadas![](https://i.imgur.com/CtvQKkB.png)
![](https://i.imgur.com/UDDTEoM.png)
-  The material advantages brought about by the use of the iron implements in Eastern UP and Bihar in 6th century BC (500-600BC) helped in the formation of large territorial states.
- Use of iron tools in agriculture produced surplus, which could be taxed by the princes to finance their military and administrative needs. 
- Thus, many *Janapadas* sprung up in the 6th century BC, the larger of which were called *Mahajanapadas.*

## Dynasties which Ruled in Magadha
1. <mark style="background-color:#d08770">Haryanka Dynasty</mark>
   - Bimbisara
      - ![](https://i.imgur.com/3acMfEz.png)
      - He built the capital city Rajgir (Girivraja), which was surrounded by five hills.
      - The Gandhara ruler of Taxila, Pukku Sati, sent an ambassador to Bimbisara.
   - Ajatshatru
      - ![](https://i.imgur.com/BPi7B2c.png)
      - *Sunidha* and *Vatsakar* Ajatashatru's diplomatic ministers.
      - **Mahashilakantaka** a war engine, which catapulted big stones.
      - **Rathamusala** a kind of chariot with a mace; helped him to defeat the Lichchhavis.
   - Udayin
      - He built the fort upon the confluence of the Ganga and the Son rivers at Pataliputra (Patna), thus,![](https://i.imgur.com/SHxQ0M0.png)
