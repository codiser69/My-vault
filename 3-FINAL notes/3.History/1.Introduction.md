- History is the study of past events. The present human form is the result of gradual [[2. universe &  Evolution !!!!#^5b3e10| evolution]]. 
- The word history comes from the ancient Greek word ‘historia’, meaning 'inquiry/research/investigation', Thus, the knowledge acquired by investigation is known as  'historia'
- *Herodotus* first used the word history in his book ‘Historica’. therefore, considered as the ‘**Father of History**’
Historians have classified history into many parts for ease of study. 
- ![](https://i.imgur.com/90DWwxT.png)![](https://i.imgur.com/UkPdlbH.png)
1. [[Index of HiSTORY#^242cb7|Ancient India:]] This period covers the time from the earliest human settlements in the Indian subcontinent to around 700 CE. It encompasses the <u>Indus Valley Civilization</u>, <u>the Vedic period</u>, the rise and fall of empires like <u>the Mauryan</u> and <u>Gupta empires</u>, and the <u>development of Hinduism</u>, <u>Buddhism</u>, and <u>Jainism.</u> 
2.  [[Index of HiSTORY#^f33e9d|Medieval India:]] This period spans from around 700 CE to 1757 CE. It includes the <u>Delhi Sultanate</u>, <u>the Mughal Empire</u>, and various regional kingdoms. This period saw the <u>influence of Islam</u>, the development of new languages and literature, and significant advancements in art and architecture.
3. [[Index of HiSTORY#^97629a|Modern India:]] This period begins with the arrival of the <u>British East India Company</u> in the 17th century and extends to the present day. It covers the <u>British colonial rule</u>, the <u>Indian independence movement</u>, and the <u>formation of independent India</u>. This period witnessed significant social, political, and economic changes, including the Indian independence struggle led by Mahatma Gandhi and the subsequent establishment of a democratic republic.
