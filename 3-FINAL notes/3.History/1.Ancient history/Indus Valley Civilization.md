## Nomenclature of Indus Valley Civilization
- ![](https://i.imgur.com/CJzbiZu.png)
- Location of  Taxila (Takshshila) is found by Alexander Cunningham
- Harappan Civilization after the first discovered site, Harappa.
- *Dayaram Sahni* first discovered **Harappa** in 1921.

- **Saraswati-Sindhu Civilization** as most of the sites have been found along the Indus-Saraswati river.
